1. THE ANATOMY OF A PERFECT PROMPT (ORDER OF IMPORTANCE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Prompts must be built sequentially from left to right. Early tokens carry the 
most mathematical weight in the generation process.

[Tokens 1-3]  --> CORE SUBJECT: Age, explicit attire, material texture, action.
[Tokens 4-5]  --> ENVIRONMENT: Micro-details of the scene, weather, depth layers.
[Tokens 6-7]  --> LIGHTING: Light source, angle, temperature, shadow behavior.
[Tokens 8-9]  --> CAMERA & LENS: Focal length, f-stop, camera type, film stock.
[Tokens 10+]  --> ENGINE CODE: Stylistic directives, rendering parameters.

3. ATMOSPHERIC & LIGHTING COMMAND CODES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Lighting determines the structural integrity of shadows. Never just say "light."
Inject these specific lighting codes to enforce depth:

• "Volumetric Chiaroscuro Lighting" — Forces extreme contrast between dark/light.
• "Rim Lighting, 45-degree Key Light" — Separates the subject from the background.
• "Dappled Sunlight through Foliage" — Creates organic, complex shadow maps.
• "Neon Ambient Spill with Volumetric Fog" — Perfect for cinematic/cyberpunk depth.
• "Golden Hour, Soft Diffused Directional Light" — Gives smooth, natural skin tones.

4. ENGINE-SPECIFIC SYNTAX SHORTCUTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tailor the tail-end of your text file based on the engine you are running:

■ MIDJOURNEY v6 PARADIGM:
  Always append `--style raw` to disable Midjourney's default "beautification" 
  and force it to stick to your exact literal words. Set aspect ratios using `--ar`.
  Example Template: [Subject], [Environment], [Camera], [Lighting] --style raw --ar 16:9

■ STABLE DIFFUSION (SDXL) PARADIGM:
  Use explicit weights `(token:1.2)` to emphasize physical textures over concepts.
  Example: "Close-up shot of an elder man, (intricate skin pores:1.3), raytracing."

■ DALL-E 3 PARADIGM:
  DALL-E 3 requires descriptive paragraphs rather than fragmented phrases.
  Example: "A candid medium-format photograph of... The lighting is a sharp rim 
  light coming from behind. Captured on a 35mm camera, showing realistic grain."
